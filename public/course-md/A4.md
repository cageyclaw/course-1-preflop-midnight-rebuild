# 4‑Bet and Beyond

## Objective
Know **when to 4‑bet, when to call, and when to fold** so you don’t torch stack depth in bloated pots.

## Definitions
- **4‑bet:** Re‑raise versus a 3‑bet.
- **Blocker:** A card that reduces opponent’s premium combos (e.g., A♠ blocks AA/AK).
- **SPR:** Stack‑to‑pot ratio; low SPR = easier to commit.

## Rules
1. **Value 4‑bet tight:** QQ+, AK as default.
2. **Bluff 4‑bet with blockers** (A5s/A4s) when you expect folds.
3. **Size to shape SPR:** ~2.2–2.5x IP, ~2.5–3x OOP.
4. **If you hate the jam call, don’t 4‑bet.**

![Vs 3-bet decision tree](/course-md/vs-3bet-decision-tree.svg)

## Exceptions
- **Vs maniacs:** widen value 4‑bets and call down more.
- **Short stacks:** simplify to 4‑bet jams with value + blockers.
- **ICM pressure:** tighten 4‑bet bluffs near bubbles.
