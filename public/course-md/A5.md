# Play Against Opens

## Objective
Build a clear response tree versus opens: **3‑bet, call, or fold** based on position and opener tendencies.

## Definitions
- **Defend:** Continue versus an open (call or 3‑bet).
- **Squeeze:** 3‑bet after an open and one or more callers.
- **Isolation:** Raising to play heads‑up versus a weaker opener.

## Rules
1. **Defend widest in the BB.** You already have money in and close action.
2. **Call more IP, 3‑bet more OOP.** Position dictates leverage.
3. **Avoid cold‑calling too wide OOP.** That’s a leak factory.
4. **Squeeze bigger** when there are callers in between.

![Squeeze risk lane](/course-md/squeeze-risk-lane.svg)

## Exceptions
- **Weak openers:** widen value 3‑bets and isolate larger.
- **Multiway pots:** tighten speculative calls OOP.
- **High rake / low edges:** favor 3‑bet or fold rather than flatting.
