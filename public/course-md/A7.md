# Tournament Dynamics

## Objective
Apply preflop fundamentals under **ICM and payout pressure** so you don’t torch EV when the money matters.

## Definitions
- **ICM:** Independent Chip Model; chips are not worth the same near pay jumps.
- **Bubble:** The stage right before payouts begin.
- **Pay jump:** The increase in prize money between places.

## Rules
1. **Early stages = chip EV.** Play closer to cash‑game ranges.
2. **Mid stacks tighten near bubbles.** Your survival has extra value.
3. **Big stacks apply pressure.** Use position and leverage to steal.
4. **Short stacks take clean spots.** Don’t pass profitable jams.

![30-day practice map](/course-md/30-day-calendar.svg)

## Exceptions
- **Bounty formats:** widen value hunting; chip EV rises.
- **Satellite events:** survival is king; fold more marginal spots.
- **Skill edge tables:** you can pass thin spots and wait for clearer EV.
